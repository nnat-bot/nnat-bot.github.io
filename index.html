80th ANNIVERSARY OF VIETNAM'S NATIONAL DAY

As September 2, 2025 draws near, Hanoi becomes a lively scene filled with red flags, ceremonial marches, and a strong sense of national pride. This year marks the 80th anniversary of Vietnam's Independence Day, blending historical remembrance with modern festivities. For visitors, this is more than just a public holiday, it's a meaningful experience that offers a deeper connection to Vietnam's culture and history.This guide provides all the essential information from key events and travel tips to cultural activities to help you enjoy a memorable National Day celebration in Vietnam.

Over the past 80 years, Vietnam has continuously developed, overcome challenges, and affirmed its position on the international stage. On this special occasion, Vietnam Travel Group extends heartfelt congratulations to all Vietnamese people, may you always remain united, joyful, and proud of your homeland.

NEW VISA EXEMPTION POLICY

Vietnam exempts visas under the Tourism Development Stimulus Program for citizens of 12 countries: Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Czech Republic, Hungary, Luxembourg, Netherlands, Poland, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia and Switzerland

“On August 8, 2025, the Government of Vietnam issued Resolution No. 229/NQ-CP on visa exemption under the Tourism Development Stimulus Program for citizens of 12 European countries. This policy will be implemented from August 15, 2025 to August 14, 2028. Accordingly, Vietnam exempts visas for citizens of the following countries: Kingdom of Belgium, Republic of Bulgaria, Republic of Croatia, Czech Republic, Hungary, Grand Duchy of Luxembourg, Kingdom of the Netherlands, Republic of Poland, Romania, Republic of Slovakia, Republic of Slovenia and the Swiss Confederation for a temporary stay of 45 days from the date of entry for tourism purposes, regardless of passport type, on the basis of fully meeting the entry conditions as prescribed by the Vietnamese law,..“
